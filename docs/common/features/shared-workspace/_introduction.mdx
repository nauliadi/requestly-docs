


Shared Workspace allows you to collaborate with your team and work together in real-time on your rules. 

## Benefits of Shared Workspace

- Seamless collaboration platform
- Real-time modification of rules
- Immediate synchronization with team
- Updated rules for everyone
- Enhanced productivity

![Introduction To Workspace](/img/docs/intro-to-ws.png)

## Features

Working in a workspace helps you share your debugging workflows with your team mates in real time. Members in a workspace can collaborate on building :
- Rules
- Mock servers
- Shared lists
- Sessions Recordings
